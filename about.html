About PKL

PowderKeg Labs (PKL)

PKL is a JavaScript software engineering consultancy specializing in rapid, iterative, feedback-driven, and data-driven development. We believe in the Lean methodology of "launch fast, iterate faster". Our goal is to deploy a version of your product, with only key features, as quickly as possible (the Minimum Viable Product). Then rapidly delivering new features on a continuous release cycle depending on user data and feedback.

Our style of development allows you to really understand which features are important to your business, thereby reducing project costs and maintaining your budgets.


Your Tech Team

PKL is your Chief Technology Officer and highly-skilled Agile development team. Along with being expert JavaScript developers, we provide strategic technical guidance to help your organization grow and succeed.


The Anti-Consultancy Model

Fair warning, we take a practical, rational, and value-centric approach to our work. We look out for what's actually in your best interests. This means expressing our opinions regarding features we believe are unnecessary or should be deferred.

In fact, we'll often tell you to cut​ features instead of add them.

It’s an “anti-consultancy model”; developers typically push clients to add more features which translates to higher revenues for themselves. That's not what we do, we question whether a feature will bring you value. And you can use us as little or as much as you want.

Our clients seem to appreciate this - take a look at our testimonials. Because of this approach, we are selective about who we work with.
