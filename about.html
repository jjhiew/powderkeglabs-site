About PKL

PowderKeg Labs (PKL)

PKL is a JavaScript software engineering consultancy specializing in rapid, iterative, feedback-driven, and data-driven development. We believe in the Lean methodology of "launch fast, iterate faster". Our goal is to deploy a version of your product, with only key features, as quickly as possible (the Minimum Viable Product). Then rapidly delivering new features on a continuous release cycle depending on user data and feedback.

Our style of development allows you to really understand which features are important to your business, thereby reducing project costs and maintaining your budgets.
